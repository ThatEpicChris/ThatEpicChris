2018 SHOW SHIRT
The previous year's shirt was a hit, and the Marching Trojans knew that. They reached out with much interest in having me design a shirt revolving around this season's theme of James Bond. To someone who very much enjoys James Bond films, it was a request that I could not pass up. Key elements of the shirt design included nods to the many films, a 007-esque 'logo', and a well-hidden skull (a nod to the recent Spectre film).

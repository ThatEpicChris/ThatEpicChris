Hi, I'm Chris
Hello there, I'm Chris Bravata! I am a Milwaukee-based creative studying at UWM for Digital and Visual Communications (aka Graphic Design). In my free time I love to design, code, cook, film and take photos. When possible, I also enjoy visiting Apple Stores, Whole Foods Markets, and Chicago. Regarding my design skills, I specialize in branding, videography, and UI design. I am currently learning the plethora of options within After Effects, as well as improving my organic drawing abilities.
